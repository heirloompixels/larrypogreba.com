Bladesmithing

Knives

Damascus blades forged from hand-made steel, with rosewood-burl handles and mokume guards.

I always have an assortment of knives available. Please call me at 406.285.6556 or email for current inventory. These photos are representative of what I make — I don’t always have all of them on hand. Shipping runs right around $10.

Most are Damascus belt knives: nickel-Damascus veneer with a tool-steel Damascus cutting edge, rosewood-burl handles, some with a mokume guard. Prices generally run $300–$500.
