A partial list of the players who've taken home a Pogreba instrument over the years.

- **Box Car Bob** (8 guitars)
- **Bad Rad – John Radtke** (3 guitars)
- **Jackson Browne** (4 guitars) — written up in [Fretboard Journal #22](https://www.fretboardjournal.com/features/fretboard-journal-22-whats-inside/)
- **Stephen Bruton** (1 guitar)
- **Cindy Cashdollar** (1 guitar)
- **Don Debacke** (1 guitar) — Colorado guitarist with Judy Roderick; has my 1st metal resonator
- **Dobro Dick** (1 guitar) — known around Montana and the Caribbean
- **Jerry Douglas** (1 guitar) — has played on over 1,500 albums. See his [website](https://jerrydouglas.com/)
- **Danny Flowers** (1 guitar) — Nashville player who's played with Eric Clapton, Emmylou Harris, Willie Nelson
- **Oystein Greni** (3 guitars) — of BigBang, "Norway's Rolling Stones"
- **Henry Kaiser** (3 guitars) — Museum of Making Music
- **Bobo Golem – Soylent Greenberg** (1 guitar)
- **Emmylou Harris** (1 guitar)
- **Greg Leisz** (1 guitar) — studio musician who's played with everyone; has my 1st wood resonator
- **Sonny Landreth** (1 guitar) — great slide guitarist; has played with Bonnie Raitt, Eric Clapton, Mark Knopfler, Eric Johnson, Joe Satriani, Vince Gill, Jimmy Buffett
- **Gary Nicholson** (1 guitar) — Nashville songwriter; songs cut by Bonnie Raitt, Waylon Jennings, Patty Loveless, Vince Gill, John Mayall, Neil Diamond, B.B. King
- **Ben Elder** (2 guitars) — LA writer
- **Michael Reese** (1 guitar) — Colorado guitarist; played with Emerson, Lake & Palmer
- **Dave Sturdeveant**
- **Bonnie Raitt** (3 guitars) — [this New York Times article](https://www.nytimes.com/2012/04/01/arts/music/bonnie-raitt-has-new-album-slipstream-and-a-tour.html) mentions the guitar I built for her
- **Bill Pullman** (1 guitar)
- **David Lindley** (5 guitars) — great slider; his [website](https://davidlindley.com/)
- **Darrell Scott** (8 guitars)
- **Martin Simpson** (1 guitar)
- **Verlon Thompson** — Nashville player and Guy Clark's musical partner
- **Luther Tatrrum** (1 guitar) — LA bluesman
- **Mike Post** (1 guitar) — LA composer
- **Pat Leonard** (3 guitars) — LA composer and producer; worked with Michael Jackson, Madonna, Pink Floyd, Jeff Beck, Jewel, Elton John
- **Scott Goldman** (3 guitars) — LA guitarist who works for the Grammys
- **Keb' Mo'** (1 guitar) — used my guitar playing "America the Beautiful" on PBS
